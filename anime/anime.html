My Favourite Anime


I Want To Eat Your Pancreas


This movie tells the story of a cheerful and happy woman named Sakura Yamauchi. She likes a boy in her class.
Because she knew her illness could not be cured she decided to spend the rest of her life doing the activities she wanted throughout her life with the man.


Weathering With You


Tokyo is currently experiencing rain showers that seem to distrupt the usual pace of everyone living there to no end.
Hodaka Morishima, who struggles to financially support himself by ending up with a job at a small-time publisher.
At the same time, the orphaned Hina Amano also strives to find work to sustain herself and her younger brother.
Both fates interwine when Hodaka attempts to rescue Hina from shady men, deciding to run away together. Hodaka discovers that Hina has a strange power which is the ability to call out the sun whenever she pray for it. With Tokyo's unusual weather, Hodaka suggests Hina to be sunshine girl who will clear the sky for other people.


Grave of the Fireflies


This movie tells about a young boy and his litter sister struggle to survive in Japan during World War II.
His mothers died of explosives released in his home and his mother also suffered serious injuries. The aunt who took care of him also drove him away
because he did not want to work to give him money and they had to live in an underground tunnel in order to survive.
