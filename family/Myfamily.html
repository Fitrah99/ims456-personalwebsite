MY FAMILY


My Parents


Both of these are my parents. On the left is my father named Ismail bin Sidik and he is 69 years old.
He is a very assertive and very punctual. He is also very concerned about tidiness and cleanliness around him.
On the right is my mother named Che Yang binti Ishak and she is 63 years old. My mother loves to plant flowers which is one of her hobbies that she often does in her spare time.
My mother is also very good at cooking and almost all the dishes are just delicious.
My parents have four grandchildren, 3 boys and one girl. They are no longer working due to age factors and spend a lot of time at home doing their favorite activities.

My Sibling


I have four siblings namely two sisters, one brother and I am the youngest.
My eldest sister named Roshasniza binti Ismail is 40 years old. She is married and has two children.
Next is my brother named Mohd Nasri bin Ismail who is 35 years old. He was also married and had a son.
The second sister is 32-year-old Norliarohana binti Ismail. She was also married and had a son.
Although my brothers and sisters are married and have their own house, they often visit me and my parents in the village.
